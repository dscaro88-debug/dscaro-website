Product Description

Fine Mono Hair Toppers - Breathable & Natural

For those women who desire to softly cover up their thinning hair areas and add extra volume to their entire hair scalp, the D.S HAIR Women Hair Topper is a perfect choice. This Fine Mono Hair Topper combines an open-weft back and sides for the best ventilation with a fine mono top for a realistic scalp appearance.

Key Features

  • 100% Indian Human Hair - Premium quality, soft and natural texture
  • Fine Mono Top - Realistic scalp appearance with hand-tied knots for the most natural look
  • Open-Weft Back & Sides - Maximum ventilation and breathability for all-day comfort
  • Under Hair at Front - Natural parting design for seamless blending
  • 4 Pressure-Sensitive Clips - Secure hold that stays firmly in place all day
  • 130% Density - Natural volume without looking overdone
  • 8 Natural Colors Available - From Off Black to Vanilla Butter Blonde
  • Customized Service Available - Colors, lengths, and density customizable

Perfect For

  • Women experiencing thinning hair or hair loss at the crown area
  • Those seeking a breathable, comfortable solution for fine or thinning hair
  • Anyone wanting instant length and volume enhancement with natural scalp appearance

How to Wear

1. Style your natural hair flat at the crown area.
2. Position the topper where you need coverage most.
3. Open the 4 clips and attach securely to your natural hair.
4. Blend your natural hair with the topper for seamless coverage.
5. Style as desired with heat tools (up to 350°F / 180°C).

Care Instructions

Use sulfate-free shampoos and conditioners. Wash 1-2 times per week or as needed. Gently detangle from ends to roots. Air dry when possible to maintain hair health. Store on a wig stand to maintain shape. Avoid excessive heat styling to prolong lifespan.
