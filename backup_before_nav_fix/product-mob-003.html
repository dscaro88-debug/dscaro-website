How to Use使用方法

📐
Step 1 — Adjust Height第一步 — 调节高度

Press the button on the shaft to adjust height so the handle aligns with your wrist when standing straight.按下杆体按钮调节高度，使站直时手柄与手腕齐平。

💪
Step 2 — Insert Forearm第二步 — 套入前臂

Slide forearm into the cuff. The cuff should sit 1–2 inches below the elbow for best support.将前臂穿入托口，托口应位于肘关节下方约2-4厘米处以获最佳支撑。

🤲
Step 3 — Grip Handle第三步 — 握住手柄

Firmly grip the ergonomic handle. Keep wrist straight and elbow slightly bent for natural posture.牢握人体工学手柄，保持手腕伸直，手肘微弯，自然站姿行走。

🚶
Step 4 — Walk Safely第四步 — 稳步行走

Move the crutch forward with each step. Place tip firmly on the ground before bearing weight.每步随手移动肘拐，先将脚头稳固接触地面再施力支撑。
