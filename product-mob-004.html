How to Use使用方法

📐
Step 1 — Adjust Height第一步 — 调节高度

Press the push-button on the shaft and slide to desired height. The handle should align with your wrist while standing straight with arms relaxed.按下杆体按钮，滑动至所需高度并锁定。自然站立手臂放松时，手柄应与手腕齐平。

🤲
Step 2 — Grip Handle第二步 — 握住手柄

Hold the ergonomic handle firmly with your stronger hand. Keep your elbow slightly bent (15–30°). The offset design puts weight directly over the quad base.用较强的手牢握人体工学手柄，手肘微弯15–30度。偏置设计使重力直接落于四脚底座中央。

🦯
Step 3 — Position Cane第三步 — 放置拐杖

Place the quad base flat on the floor, slightly to the side of your weaker leg. Ensure all 4 rubber tips contact the ground evenly for full stability.将四脚底座平放于地面，置于较弱腿侧偏外位置，确保4个橡胶脚头均匀接触地面以获最强稳定。

🚶
Step 4 — Walk Safely第四步 — 稳步行走

Move the cane forward with each step, bearing weight through the handle. The quad base stands freely — release it safely between steps without it falling.每步时将拐杖向前移动，通过手柄承重。四脚底座可独立站立，迈步间隙松手不会倒落。
