How to Use使用方法

🛏️
Step 1 — Insert Under Mattress第一步 — 插入床垫下方

Lift one side of the mattress and slide the long flat base of the rail between the mattress and the bed base (slatted frame or platform). Adjust the insertion depth so the rail stands securely upright at the bedside. The mattress weight locks the base in place.掀起床垫一侧，将护栏的长形底板插入床垫与床板（排骨架或平台）之间。调整插入深度，使护栏竖立于床侧稳固不晃。床垫自身重量会将底板牢牢压紧固定。

🔧
Step 2 — Adjust Rail Height第二步 — 调整护栏高度

If the rail has a height-adjustment mechanism, set it to a comfortable grip height — typically level with the user's hip when lying down. Ensure all locking pins or clamps are fully engaged before use.如护栏配有高度调节功能，可将扶手调至舒适抓握高度——通常与使用者躺卧时的髋部齐平。使用前确保所有锁销或夹紧装置完全卡扣到位。

🤝
Step 3 — Grip & Assist Movement第三步 — 握扶起身

To sit up, roll to the rail side, grip the top bar firmly with both hands, and push up from the mattress. To get out of bed, swing legs to the edge, grip the rail, and use it for balance while standing. Reverse for getting into bed.起身时，向护栏一侧翻身，双手握紧顶杆，借力撑起身体。下床时，将双腿摆至床边，抓住护栏保持平衡后起立。上床动作相反。

👜
Step 4 — Use Storage Pouch第四步 — 使用置物袋

Attach the included storage pouch to the rail frame. Store phone, TV remote, reading glasses, water bottle, or daily medications in the convenient pockets — everything within arm's reach without getting out of bed.将附赠的置物袋挂装到护栏框架上，将手机、电视遥控器、老花镜、水杯或日常药物存入各隔层，无需下床即可随手取用。
