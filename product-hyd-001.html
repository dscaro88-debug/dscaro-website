How the Hydration Reminder Works饮水提醒器工作原理So funktioniert der HydratationserinnererComment fonctionne le rappel d'hydratation

🔗
Step 1 — Clip On第一步 — 夹上

Simply clip the device onto any standard water bottle. Works with glass, plastic, and metal bottles from 20–50mm diameter.简单将设备夹在任何标准水瓶上。兼容玻璃、塑料、金属瓶，直径20-50mm。

🔵
Step 2 — It Activates第二步 — 自动激活

The LED starts its gentle pulse cycle immediately — blue light flashing for 3–5 seconds every 60–90 minutes. No app, no button, no setup.LED立即开始温和的脉冲循环——每60-90分钟蓝色灯光闪烁3-5秒。无需APP，无需按钮，无需设置。

💧
Step 3 — Residents Notice第三步 — 老人看到提醒

The soft blue glow catches the eye naturally. Staff also see it during routine rounds — no extra work required. No confusing apps for elderly residents.柔和的蓝光自然引起注意。工作人员日常巡视时也能看到，无需额外工作。老人无需使用任何APP。

🔋
Step 4 — Replace Battery第四步 — 更换电池

When the LED dims, twist open the back and replace the CR2032 battery. Takes 30 seconds. No tools needed.LED变暗时，拧开后盖更换CR2032电池，只需30秒，无需工具。
