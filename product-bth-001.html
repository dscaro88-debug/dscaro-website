How to Use使用方法

📐
Step 1 — Assemble & Adjust Height第一步 — 组装并调节高度

Press the push-button on each leg and slide to a comfortable seat height (43–58 cm range). Position the chair over the toilet or in the shower area. Check all four legs are fully clicked and stable before use.按下各腿按钮，将座椅调至舒适高度（43–58cm范围）。将椅子放置于马桶上方或淋浴区。使用前确认四脚均已完全咬合，稳定牢固。

🤝
Step 2 — Lower Safely onto Seat第二步 — 安全坐下

Approach from the front, grip both armrests firmly, and slowly lower yourself onto the seat. The padded armrests provide excellent support for those with weak legs or recovering from surgery.从正面靠近，双手握紧两侧扶手，缓慢坐下。软垫扶手为腿力不足或术后恢复的使用者提供有力支撑。

🚿
Step 3 — Shower or Use Toilet第三步 — 如厕或淋浴

For showering, sit comfortably while water flows around you — the frame is fully waterproof. For toilet use, the open seat design allows direct access. The removable bucket is also available for bedside use.淋浴时，舒适坐于椅上，水流可自由通过，框架完全防水。如厕时，开放式座圈可直接使用马桶。内桶亦可用于床边便凳场景。

🧹
Step 4 — Clean & Store第四步 — 清洗与存放

Remove the inner bucket and empty/rinse thoroughly. Wipe down the frame and seat with a damp cloth and mild disinfectant. Fold up the armrests and store in a compact space, or leave assembled for daily use.取出内桶，清空并彻底冲洗。用湿布蘸少量消毒液擦拭框架和座面。翻起扶手后可紧凑存放，也可保持组装状态备日常使用。
