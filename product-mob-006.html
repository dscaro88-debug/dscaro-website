How to Use使用方法

📐
Step 1 — Adjust Handle Height第一步 — 调节手柄高度

Press the height-adjustment button on each side and slide the handles to the desired level (79–95 cm). The correct height lets your elbows bend slightly (15–20°) when gripping the handles while standing.按下两侧高度调节按钮，将手柄滑至理想高度（79–95cm）。正确高度应使您站立时握住手柄时手肘微弯15–20度。

🚶
Step 2 — Walk with Support第二步 — 扶行前进

Hold both handles firmly and push the rollator slightly ahead of you as you walk. Keep the walker close to your body and within arm's reach at all times. The brakes should be released (upright) while walking.双手握紧手柄，行走时将助行器稍微推至身体前方。始终将助行器保持在身边，刹车释放（竖立）状态时行走。

🛑
Step 3 — Brake & Rest第三步 — 制动休息

Squeeze both brake handles gently to slow down. When you want to sit, push the brake levers downward to lock the wheels securely. Always ensure brakes are locked before sitting on the seat.轻轻握紧两侧手刹减速。坐下时，将刹车杆向下推以锁住车轮。坐下前务必确认刹车已锁定。

📦
Step 4 — Fold for Storage第四步 — 折叠收纳

Lift the seat panel upward to fold the frame. The rollator collapses to a compact 65 × 27 × 34 cm — fits in most car boots and storage spaces. Always remove any items from the pouch before folding.向上提起座椅面板即可折叠框架。折叠后尺寸65×27×34cm，可放入大多数汽车后备箱和储物空间。折叠前取出储物袋内物品。
