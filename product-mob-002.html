How to Use使用方法

🧍
Step 1 — Stand Upright第一步 — 站立直立

Stand or sit comfortably. No need to bend or reach down.站立或坐稳，无需弯腰或蹲下。

🎯
Step 2 — Aim at Object第二步 — 对准目标物品

Point the grabber jaw at the target item on the floor or shelf.将夹头对准地面或架子上的目标物品。

✊
Step 3 — Squeeze Handle第三步 — 握紧手柄

Squeeze the trigger handle to close the jaw and grip the item firmly.握紧触发手柄，夹头闭合牢牢夹住物品。

🙌
Step 4 — Lift & Release第四步 — 提起并松开

Lift the item to the desired height, then release the handle to drop it gently.将物品提升到合适高度，松开手柄轻放。
