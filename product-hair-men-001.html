How to Apply Your Toupee发块佩戴教程

✂️
Step 1 — Prepare第一步 — 准备

Clean and dry scalp. Trim your existing hair short. Remove any oils or products.清洁干燥头皮。将原生发剪短，去除油脂和残留物。

📐
Step 2 — Position第二步 — 定位

Place the toupee at your desired hairline. Ensure the front lace aligns with your natural hairline.将发块放置在理想发际线位置，确保前蕾丝与自然发际线对齐。

💧
Step 3 — Apply Adhesive第三步 — 涂抹胶水

Apply wig adhesive or tape along the perimeter. For glue, wait until tacky. For tape, peel and stick.沿周边涂抹粘胶或双面胶带。胶水需等待变粘，胶带直接粘贴。

✨
Step 4 — Style第四步 — 造型

Press firmly for 30 seconds. Gently blend edges with your natural hair. Style as desired.按压30秒确保粘合，轻柔地将边缘与原生发融合，即可造型。

Pro Tip:专业建议： Bleach the lace knots (2-3 times) for an even more natural look that blends seamlessly with your scalp.将蕾丝处的打结处漂染2-3次，可获得更自然的头皮融合效果。

Hair Care & Maintenance头发护理与保养

🧴 Washing Instructions清洗说明

  • • Wash every 7-10 days or when buildup occurs每7-10天清洗一次，或当有残留物时清洗
  • • Use sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner使用无硫酸盐洗发水和护发素
  • • Lukewarm water, never hot用温水，切勿使用热水
  • • Gently massage, don't rub or twist轻柔按摩，不要揉搓或拧扭
  • • Air dry on a wig stand, avoid direct heat在假发架上晾干，避免直接热源

💇 Styling Tips造型技巧

  • • Heat styling up to 350°F (180°C)可承受最高180°C热造型
  • • Use heat protectant spray使用隔热喷雾
  • • For curly styles, finger comb when wet卷发造型时，湿发时用手指梳理
  • • Store on a wig stand to maintain shape存放在假发架上保持形状

⏰ Storage存放

  • • Store on a wig stand or mannequin head存放在假发架或人头模特上
  • • Keep away from dust and direct sunlight远离灰尘和阳光直射
  • • Use silk or satin bag for travel旅行时使用丝绸或缎面袋子

🔄 Reapplication重新粘贴

  • • Remove adhesive residue with adhesive remover用去胶剂清除残留粘胶
  • • Clean base with rubbing alcohol用酒精清洁底座
  • • Let base dry completely before reapplying确保底座完全干燥后再重新粘贴
