Why Choose Thin Skin Base?为什么选择超薄皮肤底座？

Most Undetectable Base Available市面上最隐形的底座

At just 0.06mm thick, this translucent polyurethane base melts into your scalp. Even up close, it's virtually invisible. Perfect for men who want the most natural look possible.仅0.06mm厚的透明聚氨酯底座与头皮融为一体，近距离观察也几乎隐形。适合追求极致自然感的男士。

V-Looped = Zero KnotsV型钩织 = 零结点

Unlike hand-tied lace systems where knots are visible at the hairline, V-looped hair is injected through the base with a V-shaped needle. Each strand lies flat against the scalp with no bump — creating a perfectly natural appearance.与手钩蕾丝底座不同，V型钩织用V型针将头发注入底座，每根头发平贴头皮，没有凸起，外观完全自然。

Lightweight & Easy to Apply轻便易佩戴

Thin skin bases are the lightest option available. They adhere easily with tape or adhesive and are simple to clean. Ideal for first-time hair system wearers.超薄皮肤底座是最轻的选择，用胶带或粘胶即可轻松粘贴，清洁方便。特别适合首次使用发块的客户。

Natural Styling Freedom自然造型自由度

Hair can be styled in any direction — swept left, right, back, or forward — just like natural hair growing from your scalp. No part-line restrictions.头发可以向任何方向梳理——向左、向右、向后、向前——就像从头皮自然生长出来一样。没有分发限制。

How to Apply Your Hair System发块佩戴教程

✂️
Step 1 — Prepare第一步 — 准备

Clean and dry your scalp thoroughly. Shave the bonding area completely for best adhesion. Remove any oils or residue.彻底清洁干燥头皮。将粘贴区域完全剃光以获得最佳粘合效果。去除油脂残留。

📐
Step 2 — Position第二步 — 定位

Place the hair system on your head, aligning the front edge with your desired hairline. Press gently to test positioning.将发块放在头上，前缘与理想发际线对齐。轻轻按压测试定位。

💧
Step 3 — Bond第三步 — 粘贴

Apply tape or adhesive to the base perimeter. For thin skin, double-sided tape works well. Press firmly and hold for 30 seconds.在底座周边贴上双面胶或涂粘胶。超薄皮肤底座用双面胶效果很好。用力按压并保持30秒。

✨
Step 4 — Style第四步 — 造型

Blend the edges with your natural hair. Style freely in any direction. For best results, apply a thin layer of scalp concealer to the base edges.将边缘与原生发融合。向任何方向自由梳理造型。建议在底座边缘涂一层薄薄的头皮遮瑕膏效果更佳。

Pro Tip:专业建议： For thin skin bases, we recommend using Walker Ultra Hold tape or Got2b Glued for the strongest, longest-lasting bond. Always clean the base with alcohol between applications.超薄皮肤底座推荐使用Walker Ultra Hold胶带或Got2b Glued获得最强最持久的粘合效果。每次粘贴前用酒精清洁底座。

Hair System Care & Maintenance发块护理与保养

🧴 Washing Instructions清洗说明

  • • Wash every 5-7 days with sulfate-free shampoo每5-7天用无硫酸盐洗发水清洗一次
  • • Always remove before washing — don't wash while attached清洗前必须先取下，不要戴着清洗
  • • Use lukewarm water, gently massage the hair用温水，轻柔按摩头发
  • • Avoid scrubbing the base — gentle patting only不要擦洗底座——只能轻轻拍干
  • • Air dry on a mannequin head or wig stand在假发架或人头上晾干

💇 Styling Tips造型技巧

  • • Can be heat-styled up to 320°F (160°C)可承受最高160°C热造型
  • • Use low heat setting — thin skin is heat-sensitive使用低温档——超薄皮肤对热敏感
  • • Always use heat protectant spray before styling造型前务必使用隔热喷雾
  • • Avoid excessive heat — it can damage the thin base避免过度加热——可能损伤薄底座

⏰ Storage存放

  • • Store on a mannequin head or wig stand存放在假发架或人头上
  • • Keep away from direct sunlight and heat sources远离阳光直射和热源
  • • Apply adhesive remover before extended storage长期存放前先去除残留粘胶

🔄 Lifespan使用寿命

  • • Average lifespan: 3-6 months with daily wear平均寿命：日常佩戴3-6个月
  • • Thin skin is less durable than lace — expect more frequent replacement超薄皮肤不如蕾丝耐用——更换频率更高
  • • Proper care can extend to 6+ months正确护理可延长至6个月以上

Frequently Asked Questions常见问题

Q: How is thin skin different from lace?Q: 超薄皮肤和蕾丝有什么区别？

A: Thin skin (polyurethane) creates a more realistic scalp appearance with no visible knots. It's lighter and adheres more easily than lace, but is less breathable and has a shorter lifespan (3-6 months vs 12+ months for lace). It's ideal for those prioritizing the most natural look.A: 超薄皮肤（聚氨酯）比蕾丝更像真实头皮，没有可见结点。它更轻、更容易粘贴，但透气性较差，寿命较短（3-6个月 vs 蕾丝12个月以上）。适合最追求自然效果的用户。

Q: Can I shower or swim with this?Q: 可以戴着洗澡或游泳吗？

A: Swimming is not recommended — prolonged water exposure can weaken the thin skin bond. For showering, use a waterproof shower cap. The base is not breathable, so sweat buildup should be managed by removing the system periodically.A: 不建议游泳——长时间浸水会减弱底座粘合。淋浴时建议戴防水帽。底座不透气，需要定期取下以管理汗液积累。

Q: Can I dye or bleach the hair?Q: 可以染发或漂发吗？

A: Yes, since it's 100% human hair. However, be extremely cautious with heat during the process — thin skin can melt or deform under high temperatures.A: 可以，因为是100%真人发。但过程中要极其注意温度——高温可能使超薄皮肤熔化或变形。

Q: How do I remove the system?Q: 怎么取下发块？

A: Apply adhesive remover between the base and scalp. Wait 1-2 minutes for the bond to soften, then gently peel the system off from front to back. Never pull forcefully — use more remover and patience.A: 在底座和头皮之间涂抹去胶剂。等待1-2分钟让粘胶软化，然后从前往后轻轻揭下。绝不要用力拉扯——多用去胶剂，保持耐心。

Q: Do you offer custom sizes?Q: 提供定制尺寸吗？

A: Yes! We can customize base size, shape, hair density, color, length, curl pattern, and even create full custom designs. MOQ for custom orders is 10 pieces. Contact us with your requirements.A: 是的！我们可以定制底座尺寸、形状、头发密度、颜色、长度、卷曲类型，甚至全定制设计。定制订单最低起订量10件。
