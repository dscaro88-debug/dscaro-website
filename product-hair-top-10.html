Product Description

Fine Welded Custom Mono Topper

For women who want a seamless and natural-looking hairstyle, the TOP-10 Fine Welded Custom Mono Topper from D.S Hair is a high-end hairpiece. It has a naturalistic appearance and an exceptionally soft texture because it is made from 100% Chinese virgin hair.

Features

  • 100% Chinese Virgin Hair - Exceptionally soft texture and natural appearance
  • Fine Welded Mono Center with 1" NPU - Comfortable all-around perimeter for secure fit
  • 1/8" Folded French Lace Front - Seamless hairline for a natural look
  • Freestyle Hair Direction - Versatile styling in any direction
  • 120% Density - Natural fullness without looking overdone
  • 8 Natural Colors Available
  • Customized Service - Available in custom colors and lengths

Perfect For

  • Women experiencing partial hair thinning or hair loss
  • Those seeking seamless coverage with premium virgin hair quality
  • Anyone wanting natural-looking volume with fine mono construction

Care Instructions

Use sulfate-free shampoos and conditioners. Wash 2-3 times per week. Air dry when possible. Store on a wig stand to maintain shape. Avoid excessive heat styling.
