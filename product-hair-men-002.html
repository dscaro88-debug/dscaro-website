Frequently Asked Questions

How do I measure my head for the correct cap size? +
Use a soft measuring tape to measure around your head just above your ears and across your forehead. Record the measurement in inches: 21"-22" = Small, 22"-23" = Medium, 23"-24" = Large. If you're between sizes, we recommend ordering the larger size with adjustable straps.
Can I style this wig with heat tools? +
Yes! This wig is made from 100% human hair and can be straightened, curled, or blow-dried. We recommend using heat protectant products and keeping temperatures below 350°F (180°C) to maintain the hair's quality and longevity.
How long will this wig last? +
With proper care and maintenance, this wig can last 6-12 months or longer. Key factors affecting lifespan include: frequency of wear, styling methods, maintenance routine, and storage practices.
Can I wash and condition this wig? +
Absolutely. Use sulfate-free shampoos and conditioners specifically designed for human hair wigs. Wash every 7-14 days depending on wear frequency. Always detangle gently from ends to roots and allow to air dry on a wig stand.
Do you offer custom colors or sizes? +
Yes! We offer custom coloring and sizing for wholesale orders. MOQ for custom orders is typically 5 pieces. Contact us for a custom quote and production timeline.
