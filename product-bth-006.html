How to Use the No-Rinse Bathing Kit免冲洗沐浴套装使用方法

🧻
Step 1 — Warm the Wipes第一步 — 预热湿巾

Warm the body wipes in a warming cabinet or microwave for 30 seconds before use. Warm wipes are more comfortable for patients and improve cleaning effectiveness.沐浴前将湿巾放入加热柜或微波炉加热30秒。温热湿巾更舒适，清洁效果更佳。

🧴
Step 2 — Apply Foam Wash第二步 — 使用沐浴泡沫

Apply the no-rinse foam directly to skin. No water needed — the formula contains gentle cleansers that lift dirt and bacteria without rinsing.将免冲洗泡沫直接涂抹于皮肤。无需水——配方含有温和清洁成分，无需冲洗即可清除污垢和细菌。

🧤
Step 3 — Wipe Clean第三步 — 擦拭清洁

Use the large 30×60cm body wipes to thoroughly clean each body area. Work from clean to dirty areas. Each wipe is single-use for hygiene.使用大码30×60cm沐浴湿巾彻底清洁身体各部位。从干净区域到脏区擦拭。每次使用一张，确保卫生。

💧
Step 4 — Air Dry & Moisturise第四步 — 风干并涂抹润肤乳

Let skin air dry naturally (1–2 minutes). Apply the moisturising lotion included in the kit. Dress the patient. Entire process: under 10 minutes.让皮肤自然风干（1-2分钟）。涂抹套内附带的保湿润肤乳。为患者穿衣。全程：10分钟内。
