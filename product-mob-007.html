How to Use使用方法

📐
Step 1 — Unfold & Adjust Height第一步 — 展开并调节高度

Press the side release buttons to unfold the frame. Then press the push-button on each leg and slide to one of the 6 height positions. The correct height lets your elbows bend slightly (15–20°) while holding the handles upright.按下侧面释放按钮展开框架。再按各腿上的按钮，调至六档高度之一。正确高度为站立握把时手肘微弯15–20度。

🚶
Step 2 — Walk with Support第二步 — 扶行前进

Hold both handles firmly and push the frame slightly forward as you step. The 2 front wheels roll smoothly; the rubber rear ferrules grip the floor when you bear down. Take small, steady steps and keep the frame close.双手握紧把手，行走时将框架轻推向前。前轮平滑滚动；向下发力时后橡胶脚垫抓地稳固。步幅宜小、节奏均匀，框架始终保持在身边。

⏸️
Step 3 — Stop & Rest第三步 — 暂停休息

To pause, press down on the handles so the rubber rear tips contact the floor. The frame will stay stable while you rest. Do not sit on the walking frame — use a nearby chair or seat for resting.需要暂停时，向下压把手使后橡胶脚垫接触地面，框架即可稳定站立。休息时请勿坐在助行架上，请使用附近的椅子。

📦
Step 4 — Fold for Storage第四步 — 折叠收纳

Squeeze the folding latch on the side, then fold both sides inward. The frame collapses to a slim profile, fitting easily in a car boot or hallway cupboard. Always check the latch is secure before use after unfolding.按压侧面折叠扣，将两侧向内合拢，框架即折叠为扁平状，可轻松放入汽车后备箱或走廊柜中。每次展开使用前，请确认锁扣已牢固卡合。
