How to Use使用方法

📦
Step 1 — Unfold & Lock第一步 — 展开并锁定

Pull the four sections apart along the elastic cord. Each section clicks firmly into place. Ensure all joints are fully locked before applying weight.沿弹力绳拉开4段杆体，每节咔哒声确认锁定。施力前确保所有接节完全锁紧。

📐
Step 2 — Adjust Height第二步 — 调节高度

Press the push-button and slide the lower section to your ideal height (85.1–95.3 cm). Release the button to lock. The handle should align with your wrist while standing relaxed.按下调节按钮，将下段滑至理想高度（85.1–95.3cm）后松开锁定。自然站立时手柄与手腕齐平。

🖐️
Step 3 — Grip & Walk第三步 — 握杖行走

Hold the Derby handle firmly in your stronger hand, elbow slightly bent (15–30°). Move the stick forward with each stride, keeping the rubber ferrule flat on the ground.用较强的手握住德比手柄，手肘微弯15–30度。每步将手杖前移，保持橡胶脚垫平贴地面。

🎒
Step 4 — Fold & Store第四步 — 折叠收纳

Press all joints inward to collapse the four sections together. The elastic cord keeps all pieces connected. Fits in a bag or luggage — perfect for travel.向内按压所有接节，将4段折叠收拢。弹力绳保持各段相连，可放入背包或行李箱，旅行必备。
