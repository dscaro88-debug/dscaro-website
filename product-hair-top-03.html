Product Description

Women's Hair Toupee from Bono Hair, a high-end hairpiece specifically constructed for those women who are looking for a long-lasting and natural-looking hair replacement option. With a mono toupee with NPU perimeter, this system will offer outstanding breathability and a snug fit.

Key Benefits:

  • Natural Hairline: Invisible and seamless blending with your own hair
  • Breathable Design: Mono center allows excellent airflow to scalp
  • Secure Fit: NPU perimeter provides extra comfort and durability
  • Versatile Styling: Can be heat-styled, colored, and dyed
  • Customization Available: Color and length can be customized to your needs

Care Instructions:

Gentle shampooing and conditioning recommended. Air dry preferred. Use heat protection products when styling. Store on a wig stand when not in use.
